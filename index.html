About Me

I grew up on the Northern Beaches, Sydney, Australia, but now I call Seattle, Washington home.

I completed my Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies, summa cum laude, from the University of Washington and my undergraduate degree in Leisure Management from the University of Technology Sydney. I have worked in GIS since 2009, first at a large Seattle area travel company, then in various geospatial business intelligence and technical roles.

With a Master of Science in geospatial technology, I am equipped with a strong foundation in spatial analysis, remote sensing, GIS, and geospatial data management, enabling me to navigate the interdisciplinary nature of this field.

My overarching goal is to leverage my expertise and skills to contribute to the advancement of geospatial technology and its applications in various sectors. I aspire to work at the intersection of technology, data science, and geospatial analysis, where I can integrate diverse datasets, leverage cutting-edge tools and techniques, and provide valuable insights to inform decision-making processes.
